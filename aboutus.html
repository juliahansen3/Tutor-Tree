Mission Statement:

Here at TutorTree, we strive for growth. We believe that a learning difference is like a tree. What that means is that although typical LDs like ADHD and Dyslexia are big labels, it does not mean someone should feel restricted to that label. Each learning difference differs per person, who we see as a tree, with smaller branches, leaves and twigs. By zoom tutoring and pairing up 1st-8th grade students with high school or college tutors who face similar learning differences and daily hardships, we hope to create a safe environment that increases motivation for studies and promotes student confidence in regard to their learning differences and mental health.
